About Me

I’m a Master’s student in Bioinformatics at Georgia Tech, with research spanning:

  • LLM & NLP extraction of MoRF triggers
  • Deep learning for residue-level ligand binding
  • Graph-based immune repertoire clustering

My goal is to build generative and predictive AI tools for next-gen therapeutics and precision medicine.

Research Pipeline

1. Literature Mining

Use LLMs & regex to extract MoRF triggers and secondary-structure mentions from abstracts.

2. Database Curation

Clean and classify triggers into 8 molecular bins; curate structure transition categories.

3. Model Development

Train BiLSTM + PLM fine-tuned dual-head networks for disorder & ligand-binding at residue level.

4. Validation & Deployment

Evaluate on held-out BioLiP data; present at conferences; integrate into lab pipelines.
